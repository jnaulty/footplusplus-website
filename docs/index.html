An affordable FES device for footdrop treatment

Foot++ is developing an affordable Functional Electrical Stimulation (FES) device for treatment of footdrop (PENDING FDA 510K APPROVAL). We have created an accessible, affordable, and customizable FES device for footdrop as an alternative to the extremely expensive devices available on the market today. Q: How much would Foot++ improve my walking?

A: Everybody has different reactions to stimulation and symptoms, so it would depend on your personal characteristics. However, studies have shown a significant increase of walking speed and comfort.

Q: Is Foot++ right for me?

A: Since our device uses functional electrical stimulation (FES) on large muscle groups in the leg, a variety of neuromuscular diseases such as stroke, multiple sclerosis, Lumbar Degenerative Disease, muscular dystrophy, and others that cause foot drop could all be potentially tested using Foot++. Please contact us at info@footplusplus.com explaining your specific case and someone from our team will get back to you to set up a date and time to test!
